### 引语

To see the world,
things dangerous to come to,
to see behind walls,
to draw closer,
to find each other and to feel.
That is the purpose of LIFE.

开拓视野
冲破艰险
洞悉所有
贴近生活
寻找真爱
感受彼此
这就是人生的目的。
《白日梦想家》
